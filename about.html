The Morning Mission is a youth-run, officially registered non-profit organization in BC. Our teamâ€™s main goal is bridging the disconnection between the youth and the elderly through running connective and meaningful events, creating weekly educational posts, and reaching out to different elementary schools in hopes of collaboration. By allowing teens to connect with the elderly safely during these trying times, we are one step further in forming bonds and personal relationships between both demographics that will last a lifetime. Together, we can help our seniors stay connected and ingrained in our community. Let's take care of the elderly the way they took care of us when we were young.

Our Mission

Fostering meaningful relationships

Tackling society's negative perceptions and assumptions on senior citizens

Caring for the elderly through various forms of connective and enjoyable events

How will we achieve our goal?

The Buddy-Up System

Pairing up with a senior based on personality and similar interests. Learn about their past and life lessons while forming meaningful bonds along the way!

Morning Media

Spreading the older generations' knowledge to youths by conducting thought provoking interviews to later share.

Hands-on Events

Hosting ineractive events to liven up local senior homes and fundraising for our important nursing homes.
