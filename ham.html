DAY - Hamilton Distribution

The Morning Mission's event at Hamilton Village Care Centre was an incredibly uplifting experience. With a microphone passed around the room, each senior took turns sharing personal stories of their past, their jobs, and their life advice with the entire group. This allowed us to properly honour each individual, highlighting the incredible depth of life experiences and wisdom that seniors possess. Overall, the event was a beautiful reminder of the importance of celebrating and honouring the stories of our elders, and the laughter shared throughout the day brought us all closer together!
